## About

This micro blog publishes concise commentary and original analysis on governance, anti-corruption, internal control, ethical leadership, accountability, and institutional decision-making.

## Author

Mike J. Masoud is a senior director for the Middle East and Africa at The American Anti-Corruption Institute (AACI). This micro blog reflects his professional commentary, analysis, and writing on governance and corruption-related risks affecting institutions and decision-makers.
